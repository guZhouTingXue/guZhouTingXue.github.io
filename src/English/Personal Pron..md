## 用法
1. 通常用于前指 (Anaphoric Reference) - 指代前面出现的名词
- Is **the city** much different from what **it** was ten years ago?

2. 某些情况也可用于后指 (Cataphoric Reference)
- Try as **he** would, **Andrew** could not get Christine out of his mind.
- **He** **who** gains time gains everything. :代指后面gains time的人

3. we、you、they 可以用来泛指
- **We** are all apt to believe what **we** desire.

4. it 用于固定搭配，没有具体含义
 
| 用法                           | 例句                                      | 含义  |
| ---------------------------- | --------------------------------------- | --- |
| as chance would/will have it |                                         | 凑巧  |
| as it were                   | He is, as it were, a walking dictionary | 可以说 |
