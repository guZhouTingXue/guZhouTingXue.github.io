At the age of sixteen he qualified for a place at the University of Hamburg.
句子中的谓语动词是 `qualified` ? 但是qualified 只有形容词性。
qualified for sth：具有 sth 的资质/符合 sth 的条件
这里是省略了be动词？
完整的应该是he was qualified for 。。。如：Edward is well qualified for this job.
